My favorite artist is TheFatRat.

Christian Friedrich Johannes Buttner, also known as TheFatRat, didn't start making music until 2001, and was born in 1979. In 2001, he was making music under his own name, and was not known as TheFatRat. TheFatRat was used in 2011, because as a child, people in his community would call him that. After a while, he made a hit song, Unity, which started to be featured in millions of videos on the Internet. In 2020, content was made about him, including documentaries. Soon, many popular game developers started using his music in their games. Both of these factors have increased his popularity exponentially. In the present, he releases about 3 to 5 songs every year.
